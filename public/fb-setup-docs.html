Facebook Webhook Setup Guide

How to connect your Facebook Page to Blockscom for AI-powered automated replies.

Why do we need this information?

To allow the Blockscom AI to read messages sent to your Facebook Page and reply to them automatically, we need establishing a secure connection (Webhook) between Facebook's servers and ours. The Page ID tells us which page is receiving the message, the Verify Token ensures the connection is actually coming from Meta, and the Page Access Token grants the AI permission to send a reply back as your Page.

1 How to get your Facebook Page ID

Your Facebook Page ID is a unique numeric string that identifies your specific page on the platform. We need this to know exactly which page the AI should listen to.

  1. Log in to Facebook and switch to your Page profile.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  3. In the left sidebar, click on New Pages Experience (or simply About depending on your page version).
  4. Click on Page Transparency or look at the bottom of the About tab to find your Page ID.
  5. Copy the numeric sequence (e.g., 123456789012345) and paste it into the Dashboard.

2 How to set up the Verify Token

The Verify Token is a custom "secret password" that you create. When Facebook tries to connect to Blockscom, it will send this token so our system knows the request is legitimate.

  1. Go to the Meta for Developers portal and select your App.
  2. In the left menu, add the Messenger product if you haven't already.
  3. Under Messenger settings, find the Webhooks section and click Setup Webhooks.
  4. When prompted for a Callback URL, enter the Blockscom webhook URL (provided by support).
  5. For the Verify Token, invent a secret phrase (e.g., blockscom_secure_2026).
  6. Paste this exact same secret phrase into the Verify Token field in your Blockscom Dashboard.

3 How to generate a Page Access Token

The Page Access Token is essentially a temporary "key" that grants the Blockscom AI permission to reply to users on behalf of your Facebook Page. Without this, the AI can read messages but cannot send replies back.

  1. In the Meta for Developers portal, go to your App's Messenger > Settings.
  2. Scroll down to the Access Tokens section.
  3. Click Add or Remove Pages and authenticate with your Facebook account to link the page you want to automate.
  4. Once the page is linked, click the Generate Token button next to your page name.
  5. A popup will appear with a long string starting with EAAb.... Copy this token immediately (Facebook will not show it to you again!).
  6. Paste this token into the Page Access Token field in your Blockscom Dashboard.
